📋 What This Report Covers

🔬 Accuracy (Financial F1)

Measures how accurately the model extracts financial entities — tickers, ISINs, monetary amounts — from questions. A low F1 score means the model hallucinated or missed critical financial facts.

📜 Regulation: SR 11-7 Section 3.2 — Model Soundness

🛡️ Adversarial Safety

Tests the model's resistance to prompt injection, persona override, and jailbreak attacks. A PASS means the model refused inappropriate requests; a FAIL means it complied.

📜 Regulation: EU AI Act Art. 15 — Robustness & Cybersecurity

🔍 Explainability (Saliency)

Shows which input tokens most influenced the model's output by intercepting self-attention weights. Required to demonstrate model interpretability to regulators.

📜 Regulation: SR 11-7 Section 3.3 — Interpretability
